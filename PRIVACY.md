# Packet Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

## Information We Collect
Packet collects and stores your VPN configuration details (Server URL, Shared Secret) **only on your local device**. This information is used strictly to establish and maintain your network connection.

## Data Sharing
Packet does not share any user data with third parties. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

## Traffic Metadata
When you are connected, your configured server receives the traffic and connection metadata required to establish and operate the tunnel. Packet itself does not log or monitor your traffic.

## Your Choices
You can disconnect the tunnel at any time through the app or iOS System Settings. Deleting a server profile in the app removes the configuration data from your device.
