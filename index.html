Transparency First

We don't know you.

Packet is designed so that we simply cannot collect data about you. That is a feature, not an oversight.

  • ✓ Configuration stored on-device only. Your server URL and shared secret never leave your phone.
  • ✓ No account required. We don't collect your name, email, or any personal information.
  • ✓ No analytics or trackers. The app contains zero third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.
  • ✓ Traffic goes through your server. We operate no shared VPN servers and have no visibility into your traffic.
