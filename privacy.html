Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Short version: Packet is designed to collect as little data as possible. Your VPN configuration is stored only on your device. We don't have servers that log your activity, and we don't sell or share any data.

1. Information We Collect

Packet only requests the information needed to establish and maintain your network connection. This includes:

  • Server URL: The endpoint of your configured server, stored locally on your device.
  • Shared Secret: The authentication credential for your server, stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage APIs.
  • VPN Disclosure Acknowledgement: A boolean flag stored on-device to track if you have reviewed the required iOS VPN disclosure.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The configuration data stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Establish and maintain your VPN tunnel connection when you choose to connect.
  • Restore your last used configuration when you reopen the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with any third parties. Packet does not contain any advertising SDKs, marketing SDKs, or analytics frameworks.

When you connect, your configured server receives the traffic and connection metadata required to operate the tunnel. We do not operate or control any shared VPN servers, and have no visibility into your network traffic.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All configuration data is stored locally on your device. You can:

  • Delete individual server profiles inside the app's Settings screen.
  • Remove all data by deleting the Packet app from your device.

5. iOS System VPN Profile

When you connect, Packet creates an iOS system VPN configuration profile. This profile is managed by iOS and can be removed at any time via iOS Settings → General → VPN & Device Management. Deleting the app will also remove this profile.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
